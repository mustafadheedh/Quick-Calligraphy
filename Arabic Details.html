Arabic Calligraphy Details

Arabic calligraphy is the artistic practice of handwriting and calligraphy based on the Arabic alphabet. It is known in Arabic as khatt (Arabic: п«пи), derived from the word 'line', 'design', or 'construction'. Kufic is the oldest form of the Arabic script.
